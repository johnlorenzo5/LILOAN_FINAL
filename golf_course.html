ABOUT THE PLACE

Liloan, Cebu, is home to the Liloan Golf and Leisure Estate, a scenic golf course located within the town's lush and rolling landscapes. This golf estate offers an 18-hole course designed to provide both a relaxing and challenging experience for golf enthusiasts. The estate is popular not only for its well-maintained fairways but also for the beautiful views of the surrounding countryside. It is a great spot for beginners and seasoned players alike. Additionally, the facility often includes amenities such as a clubhouse, dining options, and spaces for events or gatherings.
